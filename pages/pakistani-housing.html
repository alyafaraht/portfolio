Pakistani Housing

Project

Design Studio 2023

Supervisor

Dr.Eng. Agus Hariyadi

Dr.Eng. Nedyomukti Imam Syafii

Location

Jakarta, Indonesia

Emphasize of the study

Computational design

Pakistani housing has made its way up along with the emerging and popularity of start-up business and the highly-priced land. The design of this rental office can accommodates at the least of 500 employee, which are occupied in the computationed floor amount (tower of 32 fl).

Computational design has been used since the very first design stage of the design process, which was defining the area that is allowed to be built. This stage then followed with the step of defining the floor amount as well as the tower form by using grasshopper with the government regulation and the occupancy as the parameters. The enhancements has been utilized with regard to the building performance. The design process used grasshopper and honeybee to determined the most efficient facade (the double skin facade).
